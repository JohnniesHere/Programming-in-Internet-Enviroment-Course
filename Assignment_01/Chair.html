Step 1: Base Assembly

1. Take the 5-star base (C) and insert all five castors (D) into the base holes.

2. Insert the gas lift cylinder (E) into the center hole of the base.

Step 2: Seat Mechanism Assembly

1. Place the seat mechanism (G) onto the seat cushion (B).

2. Secure the mechanism using:

  • One M6*25mm screw (M) at the front
  • One M6*20mm screw (K) at the back
Important: Please ensure the screws used are correct.

Step 3: Armrest Installation

1. Position the armrests (H) on both sides of the seat cushion.

2. Secure each armrest using:

  • M6*40mm screws (J)
  • M8*φ17*2mm washers (L)
Note: Tighten the 6 screws
Important: Please ensure the screws used are correct.

Step 4: Backrest Installation

1. Align the backrest (A) with the mounting points on the seat.

2. Secure the backrest using M6*20mm screws (K) on both sides.

Important: Please ensure the screws used are correct.

Step 5: Mechanism Adjustment

1. Once the backrest is positioned, insert and start all 6 screws.

2. Half-tighten all screws to allow for final adjustments.

Note: 6 screws are to be half-tightened at this stage

Step 6: Final Assembly

1. Install the armrest covers (I) on both sides.

2. Place the assembled seat onto the gas lift cylinder base.

3. After confirming all alignments are correct, fully tighten all screws.

Final Check: Ensure all screws are properly tightened and the chair is stable before use.
